Urban Decay

Proportional Map
This map depicts the most potential decayed cities in America. The cities are selected where there are more than 200 thousands population. Then according to the annual population growth rate, crime rate and unemployment rate, the nine cities including Detroit, Chicago, Milwaukee, Toledo, Buffalo, Cleveland, Baltimore, Memphis, and Anchorage are picked out because all of them have negative population rate, relative high crime rate (>372) and above average unemployment rate(>5.3).
